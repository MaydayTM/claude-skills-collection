# Methodology 03 — Sales & pre-qual (kort)

## De 3 vragen vóór de sale
1) Do I like this person?
2) Do you have work that I can do?
3) Can you afford me?

## Budget prompts
- “Do you have a budget? Do you mind sharing it with us?”
- “What have you spent in the past?”
- “Give me an average — I won’t hold you to it.”

## Output
- Score 0/1 per vraag + besluit: proceed / refer / decline
