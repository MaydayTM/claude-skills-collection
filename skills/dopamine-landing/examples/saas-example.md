# Example: SaaS Analytics Tool Landing Page

## Context
Product: AI-powered analytics dashboard that predicts customer churn before it happens

Target Audience: SaaS founders and product managers

## Section 1: Visual Impact (15-25 words)

**Hero Visual:** Looping video of dashboard showing churn prediction lighting up red 3 days before a cancellation

**Headline:**
"See who's leaving before they click cancel"

**Subheadline:**
"AI predicts churn 7 days early. Every time."

---

## Section 2: Curiosity Loop (30-50 words)

**Large Question:**
"Why do most SaaS companies only learn about churn after it's too late?"

**Problem Statement:**
The average founder discovers a cancellation 7 days after the customer mentally checked out.

**Teaser:**
Your data already knows who's about to leave. You just can't see it... yet.

## Why This Works

1. **Visual Impact:** The looping video of a live churn prediction creates immediate movement and relevance
2. **Curiosity:** "Why do they only learn too late?" opens a loop every SaaS founder wants closed
3. **Mystery:** The scrollytelling reveals the "invisible signals" step by step
4. **Solution:** Predictive scoring is surprising and data-backed
5. **Trust:** Real testimonials with specific numbers, founder photo, and clear CTA

**Expected Performance:**
- Scroll depth: ~75% (high investment in the story)
- Time on page: 70-90 seconds
- Conversion rate: 8-12% (vs. 2-3% typical SaaS landing pages)
